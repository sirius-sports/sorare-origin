HOW TO PLAY
How to Play
1
Collect MLB player cards
You can start with free Common cards. Cards of popular players are available on the marketplace.
2
Build a 7-card roster
Assemble a roster of 7 cards: pitchers + position players. Pay attention to starter, reliever, and closer roles.
3
Enter a Game Week (GW)
Participate in the first-half and second-half GWs each week. During the season, scores update daily as games are played.
4
All game scores within the GW are summed
In MLB, all game scores within the GW are totaled (unlike other sports). Players with more games have higher scoring potential.

TOURNAMENTS
Competition Types
Sorare MLB offers 5 types of competitions across in-season, classic, PvE, and daily formats.
— In-Season Competitions
⚾
Champion
The main competition during the MLB season. Rankings are determined by total GW scores. Limited / Rare allow up to 4 lineups; SR / Unique allow up to 2 lineups.
— Classic Competitions (Past Season Cards Allowed)
📂
Challenger
A competition where you can use cards from previous seasons. Great for collectors who may not have the latest cards.
— PvE Competitions (vs. Computer)
🔥
Hot Streak
A PvE format where you don't compete directly against other players. Hit the target score to earn rewards. Up to 3 lineups per rarity. A great mode for beginners to earn rewards.
— Daily Competitions
📅
Arena
Short-term competitions held on an irregular schedule. They conclude within 24 hours, giving you additional chances to compete outside the regular weekly GW.
✅
Daily Mission
A mission format where you can assign up to 21 players per day. Earn Essence, Credits, and XP.
💡 Multi-entry quick reference: Limited / Rare = up to 4 lineups | SR / Unique = up to 2 lineups | Hot Streak = up to 3 lineups per rarity
ESSENCE & CRAFT
Essence & Craft
Essence is the in-game currency used to enhance and create cards. You can earn it through Daily Missions and competition rewards. It can be used for the following:
UseDescription
CraftSpend Essence to create new cards
Card LevelingAccumulate XP to level up cards (max Lv.10)
Vault (Seal)Seal cards to receive specific rewards
📈 Card Level Bonus: Each level adds a +1% score bonus (Lv.0 to 10). SR and Unique cards receive an additional unique bonus at Lv.10.
VAULT SYSTEM
Vault (Seal) System
The Vault lets you seal cards you're not actively using to earn rewards over time.
FeatureDetails
Eligible CardsLimited, Rare, Super Rare, and Unique cards can all be sealed
RewardsEssence, Credits, special items, and more
ImportantSealed cards cannot be used in tournaments. Unsealing is possible
💡 Be careful not to seal cards you need for your lineup. Always check your seal status before building your GW roster.
