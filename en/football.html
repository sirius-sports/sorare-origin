WHAT IS SORARE
What is Sorare?
Sorare is a fantasy sports game where you collect digital cards of real players, build teams, and compete based on actual match results. Cards are NFTs built on blockchain technology and can be bought, sold, and traded. The game covers three sports — Football, MLB, and NBA — with official partnerships with hundreds of clubs worldwide.
HOW TO PLAY
How to Play
1
Get Player Cards
Start with free Common cards. Build your collection through market purchases and tournament rewards.
2
Build a Team of 5 Cards
Create a team with GK + DEF + MID + FWD + 1 Flex slot (any position).
3
Enter a Game Week (GW) Tournament
Participate in twice-weekly GWs. Check player schedules and matchups carefully before building your lineup.
4
Scores Based on Real Match Results
Your players' actual performances (goals, assists, ratings, etc.) are reflected in their scores.
5
Earn Rewards for Top Finishes
Win cards and prizes based on your ranking.

SCORING SYSTEM
Scoring System
Team Score = Sum of 5 Card Scores
Card Score = Player Score x Bonus Multiplier
Player Score = Decisive Score (DS) + All Around Score (AAS)
Player Scores are calculated on a scale of 0 to 100 points. Both starters and substitutes begin at Level 0 (35 pts), leveling up with positive stats and leveling down with negative stats.

COMPETITIONS
Competition Types
Sorare Football competitions are divided into three categories: In-Season, Classic, and Daily/PvE.
── In-Season Competitions (Pro Cards Required)
👑
Champion
The top-tier competition. Includes dedicated competitions for major leagues like the Premier League and La Liga. Each team is promoted/relegated independently, and you can submit up to 4 teams per GW.
🥈
Contender
The second-tier in-season competition. Less competition makes it easier for mid-level managers to earn rewards. Also covers MLS, J-League, and more.
── Classic Competitions (Past Season Cards Allowed)
⚔️
All Star
A classic competition where you can use cards from past seasons. Organized by regional groups: Europe, Americas, and Asia.
🎓
U23 (Under-23)
Only cards of players aged 23 or under (as of July 1) are eligible. A competition for fans looking to discover young talent.
── PvE & Daily Competitions
🔥
Hot Streak
A PvE format — no competing against other players. Beat the target score to earn rewards. 5 difficulty levels, progressing as you succeed. Up to 4 lines can be entered.
🏟️
Arena
A 10-player leaderboard format. No limit on entries. The top 5% qualify for the "Golden Arena" with increased rewards.
💡 Competitions open to Common cards: Common Champion and Common Contender only. Pro cards (Limited or higher) unlock competitions with cash and card rewards.
DIVISION SYSTEM
Divisions & Promotion/Relegation
Champion and Contender each have 3 Divisions (Div 1-3), with promotion and relegation based on GW performance.
MovementCondition
Div 3 → Div 2Top 30% promoted
Div 2 → Div 1Top 15% promoted
Div 2 → Div 3Bottom 30% relegated
Div 1 → Div 2Bottom 30% relegated
📌 Each team is promoted/relegated independently. You can manage up to 4 teams across different divisions.
MISSIONS
Mission Types
📅
Daily Missions
Select up to 3 players with matches that day. Earn XP (experience points) for decisive actions like goals, assists, and clean sheets. Resets daily at 09:00 CET.
🎯
Game Week Missions (Picker)
Complete GW-based objectives such as using players from specific leagues or achieving a collection score. Rewards include XP, Essence, and credits.
ESSENCE & CRAFT
Essence & Craft
Essence is a material earned through competitions and missions. Spend 1,000 Essence to draw 10 cards and select 1 through the "Craft" system to create a Pro card.

VAULT SYSTEM
Vault System
Seal cards to earn "Seal Points" and increase your Vault Level. Higher levels add a Reward Boost (up to +20%) to competition rewards.
