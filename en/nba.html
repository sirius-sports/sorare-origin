HOW TO PLAY
How to Play
1
Collect NBA player cards
You can start with free cards. A wide range of cards are available, from star players to solid role players.
2
Build a 5-card roster
Combine players from different positions to assemble a roster of 5 cards.
3
Enter a Game Week (GW) tournament
During the season, there are 3-4 games per week. Players with high activity rates have an advantage.
4
Only the highest-scoring game counts
In NBA, when a player plays multiple games within a GW, only the single best score is counted.

POINTS CAP
Points Cap System
The Points Cap is a key NBA-specific mechanic that limits the maximum Sorare score a player can contribute to your lineup. It prevents dominant superstars from single-handedly winning every tournament and adds strategic depth to roster building.
📊
How it works
Each competition has a cap value (e.g., 120pt). If a player's Sorare score exceeds the cap, their contribution is limited to the cap value. This encourages managers to build balanced rosters rather than relying on a single superstar.
👑
MVP Exception
In some capped competitions, you designate one player as your MVP. The MVP is exempt from the points cap and can score without limit, adding the full cap value on top of their score. Choose your MVP wisely — the right pick can make or break your lineup.

COMPETITIONS
Competition Types
Sorare NBA has 5 competition types across 3 categories: in-season tournaments, classic tournaments, and daily events.
── In-Season Biweekly Tournaments
👑
Champion
The top-tier in-season tournament. You can submit up to 5 entries per rarity. Aimed at top managers competing for the highest rewards.
🥈
Contender
The second-tier in-season tournament. Also allows up to 5 entries per rarity. A great competition for mid-level managers to earn prizes.
── Classic Biweekly Tournaments (Past-Season Cards Allowed)
⚔️
Challenger
A classic tournament where past-season cards can be used. Uses Cap 140 + MVP format with up to 3 entries per rarity.
🏟️
Arena
Requires Essence to enter. Uses a 10-player leaderboard format. A 5% chance of entering a "Golden Room" with double rewards. Three variants: Uncapped, Arena 140, and Arena 75.
── Daily Tournament
📅
Daily Pickup
Available on any NBA game day. Uses Cap 120 + MVP format. Only 1 entry per GW. No in-season cards required — easy to join casually.
⚠️ Common cards can only enter Common Champion, Common Contender, and Daily Pickup.

ESSENCE & CRAFT
Essence, Craft & Vault
✨
Essence
An in-game currency earned through competition rewards. Used to enter Arena tournaments and to craft new cards. Essence cannot be purchased directly — it is earned by playing.
🔨
Craft
Combine Essence with existing cards to create new cards. Crafting lets you build your collection strategically without relying solely on the market.
🔒
Vault
Lock your cards in the Vault to earn passive Essence over time. Vaulted cards cannot be used in competitions or traded while locked. Higher rarity cards generate more Essence.
NG / DNP
NG (No Game) & DNP (Did Not Play)
🚫
NG (No Game)
The player's team has no scheduled game during the Game Week. An NG player scores 0 points. Check the NBA schedule carefully before building your lineup.
🪑
DNP (Did Not Play)
The player's team had a game, but the player did not play (injury, rest, coach's decision, etc.). A DNP player also scores 0. Monitor injury reports and lineup news before Game Week deadlines.
💡 Both NG and DNP result in 0 points. Since NBA uses the best single game score, having at least one game where your player is active is critical. Players with more scheduled games in a GW offer more opportunities for a high score.
